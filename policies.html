There are certain policies regarding the rental services that our customers need to follow. Any damage or loss to the product rented will require you to pay a compensation. Rest of the details are listed below for each services particularly.

House on rent

  1. Booking a house will result an agreement of 11 months legally.
  2. Customers are required to check the condition of house before renting it and agree to terms and policies of owner.
  3. Any damage to the property at the duration of stay will be funded by tenants when they leave.
  4. Customers need to submit the id proof before staying in the house


Vehicle on rent

  1. Booking a vehicle will be a minimum of 1 month duration, however it can be extended mutually by the renter and owner.
  2. Customers are required to check the condition of vehicles before renting it and agree to terms and policies of owner.
  3. Any damage to the property for the duration of rent will be funded by renters when they return it.
  4. Customers need to submit the id proof and driving license before they rent it.


Food(lunch and dinner)

  1. Before booking a food service customers can book a trial pack.
  2. Trial pack will last for 1 week and it will be available on trial rates.
  3. Minimum duration of booking will be of 1 month
  4. Customers can view the menu before selecting the service.


Luggage space

  1. Check the luggage and if valuables are there lock it properly.
  2. Customers need to submit the id proof before storing the luggage.
  3. Any damage to the stored luggage will be covered by owner party.
